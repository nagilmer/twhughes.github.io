Things I like to do...

Music: I have always been primarily a music lover, and have been playing piano since I was a child and guitar and saxophone since high school.

  • In my earlier years, I made a series of electronic music albums for fun / out of boredom
  • I've been playing jazz guitar for a while.
  • Went through an extended phase of sonic experimentation in high school, where I hooked up effects pedals and processors to various instruments I would buy cheap on EBay (trumpets, saxophones, oboes, etc).

Athletics

  • Been addicted to surfing since age 16. You can find me rocking my Costco board at surf spots in the bay area once or twice a week.
  • Worked at a rock climbing gym for 4 years throughout my undergrad. Set routes for competitions and daily climbers, taught belay classes and goofed around a healthy amount. Still bouldering with some regularity, although my fingers can't handle as much stress as they used to.
  • Moderately active in indoor pickup soccer and playing casually.
  • Beginning Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu classes!

Travel

  • Spent 4 months traveling alone in China as an 18 year old. I had the chance to stay at a children's book factory in Dongguan, learning Mandarin and teaching English and golf to the workers and management. Then I spent the next few months traveling through GuangXi and YunNan provinces, Hong Kong, and Beijing. Unfortunately, I have forget almost all of my Mandarin at this point.
  • After graduation, I went on a soul/job searching trip through New Zealand, Singapore, Malaysia, Cambodia, and Thailand. Eventually got a job doing research at the Centre for Quantum Technologies in the National University of Singapore (NUS). Went on a few side trips with friends to Taiwan, Vietnam, and Indonesia.
  • Am fortunate to have the opportunity to travel to meetings as part of the Accelerator on a Chip (ACHIP) collaboration. In the past year, my girlfriend and I have visited Germany, Switzerland, Italy, Austria, and Hungary in the weeks after these meetings. I developed a great love of German beer, food, and beer gardens..

Food: I enjoy cooking and esecially working on fermentation projects.

  • I'm currently learning how to make sourdough bread, although I am still perfecting the art.
  • I've been brewing beer for the past 2 years. So far I have made several styles, including various IPAs and Pale Ales, Hefeweizen, Kolsh, and Imperial Stout. My next project will be a dry-hopped Gose. I am going to be brewing German Style Lagers as soon as I get the proper equiptment.
  • From highschool through undergrad, I brewed my own kombucha.
  • Other: kimchi, natto (stinky fermented Soybeans).
  • I am currently aging Pu-erh tea.
